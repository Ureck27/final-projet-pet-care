# 🖼️ Quick Start: Image Upload Feature

## What's New? 🎉

Your PetCare platform now supports **image uploads** for:
- ✅ **Pet Profiles** - Upload photos when adding/editing pets
- ✅ **Trainer Photos** - Upload profile photos during trainer application

---

## How to Use

### For Pet Owners - Adding a Pet Photo:

1. Go to **My Pets** page
2. Click **"Add Pet"** button
3. Fill in pet details (name, breed, age, etc.)
4. Scroll down to **"Pet Photo"** section
5. **Option A - Drag & Drop:**
   - Drag your pet photo onto the upload zone
6. **Option B - Click to Select:**
   - Click the upload zone
   - Choose image from your computer
7. See preview appear
8. Click **"Add Pet"** to save

---

### For Trainers - Adding Profile Photo:

1. Go to **Become a Caregiver** or trainer application
2. On **Step 1 (Personal Info)**
3. Scroll to **"Profile Photo"** section
4. Upload your professional photo:
   - Drag image onto zone, OR
   - Click to select file
5. See preview appear
6. Click **"Next"** to continue

---

## 📸 Image Requirements

- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: Up to 5MB
- **Recommended**: Clear, well-lit photos
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) works best

## Troubleshooting

### Image won't upload?
- ✓ Check file is PNG or JPG
- ✓ Check file size is under 5MB
- ✓ Try refreshing browser
- ✓ Check browser console for errors

### Image not showing in preview?
- ✓ Wait a moment for file to process
- ✓ Try uploading again
- ✓ Try different image file

### Can I upload multiple images?
- Currently: One image per pet/profile
- Future: Multiple images per pet (photo gallery)

## File Formats Supported

✅ **Supported:**
- .png (PNG)
- .jpg (JPEG)
- .jpeg (JPEG)
- .gif (GIF)
- .webp (WebP)

❌ **Not Supported:**
- .bmp, .tiff, .svg (currently)
- Large files > 5MB

## Tips & Tricks

### Best Photo Tips:
1. **Pets**: Clear shot of your pet's face/full body
2. **Trainers**: Professional headshot or action shot with animals
3. **Lighting**: Natural light works best
4. **Background**: Plain or simple background preferred
5. **Clarity**: Avoid blurry or dark photos

### Browser Tips:
- Works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- For best experience, use latest browser version
- Mobile browsers also fully supported
